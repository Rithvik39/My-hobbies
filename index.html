My Favourite Hobby
Playing Cricket



I love playing cricket. Cricket is my passion and I want to become a great cricketer.
I play cricket by myself and sometimes with my father. He too knows how to play cricket and teaches me some tricks.
Playing with my father is so fun. By practising cricket I get to know how to hit the ball by the way the bowler is bowling.
If I am bowling, I get to know how to bowl with tricks and get the batsman out. Cricket also has to do with mind.
With my mind I can think if I should hit the ball with power or hit the ball slowly. It also applies in bowling.
By mind I can know where to bowl and confuse the batsman by bowling at different places.
